Frequently Asked Questions

What is Quanta Gold?

Quanta Gold is a free online currency converter platform which lets your convert between any two currencies. We support all 161 major currencies used in 200 countries. Our currency API uses multiple sources for stable & reliable FX data. It's an easy to use free platform for checking currency rates - no personal details/billing informaton required. We do not supply buy/sell spread data and so our rates are unsuitable for forex trading or processing cross currency settlements.

How accurate is the currency data?

We fetch currency data from a trusted public API called "ExchangeRate-API". ExchangeRate-API collects exchange rate data from multiple central banks & commercial sources and then use their own algorithm to blend these different datasets. This process reduces the impact of an incorrect outlying conversion rate supplied by one source. Our exchange rates are classed as indicative midpoint rates. These are accurate enough for tasks like price estimations in an e-commerce store or stats on a dashboard.

How often do the exchange rates update?

Updated data is available every 24 hours.

Where does the API get foreign exchange rate data from?

Statement from ExchangeRate-API: "We collect public reference data from a number of central banks and commercial sources around the world. An example would be the reference rates released by the European Central Bank each day."

Where does Quanta Gold get it's currency information from?

All currency information is fetched from online sources such as Wikipedia.
